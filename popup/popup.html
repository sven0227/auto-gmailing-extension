Gmail Auto-Email Sender

Distribute emails across multiple Gmail accounts. Example: 1-5 means use accounts 1 through 5 (based on Gmail tab order).
Note: Make sure you have the Gmail tabs open before starting. Accounts are numbered in the order Gmail tabs appear.
Delay in seconds between sending each email. Default: 5 seconds. Minimum: 0.5 seconds. Recommended: 2-5 seconds to avoid triggering spam filters.
